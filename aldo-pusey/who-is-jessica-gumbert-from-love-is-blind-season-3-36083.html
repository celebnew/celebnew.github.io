Who Is Jessica Gumbert From Love Is Blind Season 3?

Publish date: 2024-04-14

Love is Blind’s first three episodes have been released, and fans are overjoyed. The program aims to aid viewers in finding true love. The objective is to determine whether people can develop deep, lasting relationships without ever physically meeting.

On Netflix’s Tulum, the show’s synopsis reads as follows:

“Get your gold goblets ready: on October 19, Love Is Blind returns for Season 3. The hosts of the dating experiment, Nick and Vanessa Lachey, will be back for the upcoming show, which will introduce viewers to individuals wanting to date and possibly get engaged “eye unseen.”

One of the 30 people who participated in the show was Jessica Gumbert, a single woman. The 30-year-old social event planner struggles to deal with people who cannot express themselves.

The first four of Love is Blind’s third season’s 12 episodes will debut on October 19 at 3 a.m. ET. On October 26, the following batch of three episodes will be made available. On November 2, the remaining episodes will be made available. The final set of episodes will be available on Netflix on November 9.

15 men and 15 women have been segregated into various cages for Season 3 of Love are Blind. One of the cast members highlighted in the season premiere was the fantastic chef Jessica Gumbert.

Jessica, also known as Jess, graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Marketing and Business Administration from Minnesota State University in 2015. She is a “creator of Unforgettable Experiences,” according to her LinkedIn profile, and she has a remarkable and varied career.

According to her LinkedIn profile, Jess is a motivated and enthusiastic professional whose curiosity inspires her to work with companies to build “revolutionary solutions.”

“I stand out from many other applicants because of my effective, creative mind, positive outlook, and desire to push myself beyond my comfort zone. I’m the kind of leader who empathizes while also inspiring change because I want to make things better for everyone.

Currently employed at Number Project as a senior event producer, Jess has also had positions as a personal chef. As a side business, Jess cooked meals for her coworkers and friends. She also has a side business in Dallas called Creations by Jess, which specializes in DIY products.

Jess visited Love is Blind and is searching for a transparent and honest person. She expresses love through “acts of service” in a relationship, such as a compromise and company. According to her, you are in the wrong relationship if you need to mimic someone else for it to work.

According to Jessica’s Love Is Blind profile,

“When someone doesn’t know what they want or doesn’t explain their intentions, it drives me crazy.”
