Biography

Thomas Vanek was born in Baden bei Wien, Austria on January 19, 1984. NHL left winger who was named to his first All Star team in 2009, representing the Buffalo Sabres. After spending the bulk of his career in Buffalo, he went on to play for the Montreal Canadiens, New York Islanders, Minnesota Wild and Detroit Red Wings. He was teammates with goalkeeper Martin Biron on the Buffalo Sabres from 2005 to 2007. He scored 57 goals in two seasons with the University of Minnesota from 2002 to 2004.
