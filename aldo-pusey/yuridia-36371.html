Biography

Yuridia was born in Mexico on October 4, 1986. Famous for her numerous Platinum and Gold-designated recordings, this Mexican vocalist is particularly known for her albums La Voz de un Angel (2005), Habla El Corazon (2006), and Nada es Color de Rosa (2009). Her most popular singles include “No Me Preguntes Mas” and “Yo Por El.” In 2011, she rekindled a romantic relationship with fellow Mexican-born pop singer Mario Domm. She rose to fame while competing in Season 4 of the Mexican reality talent competition La Academia. After finishing the contest as runner-up, she released her debut album, which quickly sold more than 1.5 million copies.
