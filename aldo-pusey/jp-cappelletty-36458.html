JP Cappelletty- Biography

JP Cappelletty was born in Toledo, OH on October 13, 1993. Also known by the stage name Rook, he became the official drummer for rapper Machine Gun Kelly. He got the job as a senior in high school and dropped out to join the rapper on his Hostile Takeover tour in 2012.

JP appeared alongside Steve O in the “Wild Boy (Remix)” video. He grew up in Toledo, Ohio, and attended Sylvania Southview High School. He’d later relocate to Los Angeles, California.
