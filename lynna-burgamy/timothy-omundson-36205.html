Timothy Omundson Bio

Timothy Omundson is an actor who has been involved in the reality entertainment industry since the late 1990s. He is most known for his roles as Eli in the Xena: Warrior Princess television series, Carlton Lassiter in the film Psych: The Movie, and Sean Potter in the film Judging Amy.

Omundson, Timothy: Family Life

Timothy Micheal Omundson was born on July 29, 1969, in Saint Joseph, Missouri, as the youngest of four children of a railway worker and a teacher. He was raised in Seattle, Washington, where his parents moved when he was 12 years old.
