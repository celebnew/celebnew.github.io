Biography

Justin Holiday was born in Los Angeles, CA on April 5, 1989. 6’6″ guard/forward who debuted in the NBA with the Philadelphia 76ers in 2013. He then joined the Golden State Warriors in 2014 and was a member of the Warriors’ 2015 NBA Championship team. While playing collegiately for the University of Washington, he was named the Pac-10 Defensive Player of the Year by Netscouts Basketball in 2010. He was co-captains with Isaiah Thomas at the University of Washington. He won the State Class IV Championship at Campbell Hall School in Studio City, California.
