Biography

Willie Taggart was born in Bradenton, FL on August 27, 1976. Football coach who has served as the head coach at Florida State, Oregon, South Florida and Western Kentucky. He has the distinction of being the first African-American head football coach hired at each of those schools. He replaced Jimbo Fisher as the head coach at Florida State. He helped lead the Manatee High School football team to a Florida state title as a quarterback. He later played at Western Kentucky, where his jersey was eventually retired.
