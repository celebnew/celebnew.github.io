Biography

Kurt Carr was born in Hartford, CT on October 12, 1964. American singer and composer of gospel music known for being the Minister of Music at The First Baptist Church of Hartford. He worked with fellow gospel singer Yolanda Adams during his time as director of the West Los Angeles Church of God in Christ Choir. He made his Broadway debut as a teenage member of the Hartford Stage Company.
