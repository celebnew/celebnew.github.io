Navia Robinson – Professional Career

When it comes to Navia’s professional career, she began as a child model. She has also appeared in commercials for Moe’s Southwest Grill, Dr.Charles Stanley’s In Touch Ministries, and Smithfield Foods, Inc. She walked the runway for rising fashion icon Jason Christopher Peters and worked in a promotional web campaign for the Boosterthon Fun Run Organization.

As a young artist, she played D’Asia in the sitcom “Being Mary Jane” when she was nine years old. The web series follows the life of a young black lady who works as a talk show host, as well as her family and friends. This web series made her a well-known child artist in the country and served as a turning point in her professional life.

She began to obtain roles in TV shows after gaining fame from the series. In 2016, she had the opportunity to work on the film ‘Nicki,’ which was directed by Nicki Minaj. Meanwhile, she rose to prominence after starring in the Disney Channel family sitcom “Raven’s Home.” She’s also a cast member of ‘The Vampire Diaries,’ where she plays Janie.
