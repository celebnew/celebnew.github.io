Career

She joined Warner Bros. Records in 2014 after working as a model and released her self-titled debut album in 2017. The album reached number three on the UK Albums Chart, and eight singles were released, including “Be the One,” “IDGAF,” and the UK number-one song “New Rules,” which also charted at number six in the United States. The album has been certified platinum in every country it has been released in. Lipa was named British Female Solo Artist and British Breakthrough Act at the 2018 Brit Awards.
