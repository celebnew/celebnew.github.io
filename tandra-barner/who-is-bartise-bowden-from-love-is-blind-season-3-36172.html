Who Is Bartise Bowden From Love Is Blind Season 3?

In the third season of Love is Blind on Netflix, which debuts on October 19, 2022, Bartise Bowden will be one of 30 hopefuls. He is looking for a pleasant relationship and works as a senior analyst for a healthcare business.

The celebrity played basketball before developing numerous ankle issues. To observe his experience on the reality dating show and to follow his journey, viewers must tune in this week.

Viewers have loved Love is Blind to pieces. Regular viewers of the show have been following the candidates’ development. Fans will just be introduced to a brand-new cast of personalities in Season 3 who will bring their personal experiences to the pod in an effort to find a potential spouse by the end of the season. When will they discover true love? Only time will tell.

The Love Is Blind actor Bartise Bowden was an athlete

The 27-year-old Dallas, Texas native will appear in Season 3 of Love is Blind as he embarks on a quest to find a compatible partner with whom he can spend the rest of his life. Bartise places laughter at the top of his list of priorities, according to his Netflix profile. He claims that he is looking for someone who can both “handle all of his jokes” and “crack some back” at him.

Bartise thinks Stephen and Ayesha Curry make the perfect couple. The biography goes on to say:

Bartise claims that he is still single because of “pickiness,” and he wants to show that gym-loving people like himself are more “worth” than just their training regimen.

Bartise participated in sports and played basketball in high school. But due to injuries, he was unable to play, so he started lifting weights about seven years ago. He spoke about his problems in an interview with BodyBuilders.com, saying:

“I was a high school athlete, and I knew my dreams of playing Division I basketball were over after injuring my ankle in a basketball game my senior year. I transferred to a D-3 school to play basketball, but I had to have another operation after breaking my second ankle and gave up the sport.

He claimed that he has found harmony between his physical and mental well-being thanks to his passion of weightlifting. When he was twenty years old, he started lifting weights. Bartise said in an interview from 2019:

“About four years ago, when I was 20 years old, I started lifting weights. I got caught up in the process. I’m addicted to improvement on a physical, psychological, and emotional level. I find it fascinating how controlling and maintaining our diets and workout routines can drastically affect our bodies.

Bartise earned a Bachelor of Arts degree and a Master of Science in Accounting from The University of Texas at Dallas, both in 2017. Prior to working as an Audit Associate in 2020 and a Senior Associate in 2021 before joining a healthcare company as a Senior Analyst, he worked as an Audit Intern and Accounting Tutor from 2017 to 2018.
